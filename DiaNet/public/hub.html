Diabetes Burnout

Diabetes Burnout is the feeling of being "Done" with diabetes, feeling sick and tired of everything you need to do to manage your diabetes or if you don't care and just want to give up

Unfortunately, Diabetes Burnout is common and comes because of diabetes being a "24/7" condition with no breaks. People find it difficult to cope with all the hard work

Symptoms can include:

  • Feeling over-whelmed or angry with Diabetes

  • Feeling controlled by Diabetes

  • Feeling alone with Diabetes

  • Avoidance of diabetes management activties
