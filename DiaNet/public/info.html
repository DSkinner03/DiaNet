DiaNet

What is DiaNet

DiaNet is a way for diabetics to get all the information and resources they need online, including youtube channels, stores, helplines and more

Why was DiaNet made?

DiaNet was made because many people have a bunch of tabs open for information, and we felt there was an easier way to get all the information you need

FAQ

How does DiaNet benefit me?

DiaNet can benefit you by giving you and easy way to access the resources and information and stores you need all in one place.

When do you expect DiaNet to be out of beta

We expect DiaNet to be out of beta during mid 2021

Who can join DiaNet

If you have been invited to test and you are a diabetic you can join using the link that was sent to you
